amoCRM: Sales Team in the System in Two Weeks

amoCRM is in the top three of the Russian CRM market and is strong in exactly one thing: sales team speed. Minimal extras, fast start, messengers right in the deal card. We implement amoCRM in 1–2 weeks, from pipeline and channels to migration and training, and honestly say when you need another platform.

Estimate amoCRM implementation Ask a question

Who amoCRM fits

Sales teams of 2–20 people with short deals and active messenger chat: services, online schools, agencies, retail with requests. The platform is built for pipeline and speed, so it sticks more easily than heavy systems.

It does not fit companies that need tasks, documents, and a portal for all departments; there Bitrix24 is stronger. Vendor-neutral platform comparison is on the CRM implementation page.

Launch in 2 weeks: stages

Standard SaaS CRM setup on the market takes 1–2 weeks:

  1. Pipeline for your stages. Fields, permissions, auto-tasks.
  2. Channels into CRM. Website, telephony, Telegram, WhatsApp.
  3. Base migration. From Excel or an old CRM, with duplicate cleanup.
  4. Training on live deals. And discipline control the first weeks.

Channels into CRM

Requests from the website land in the pipeline with source tags, calls are written into the card, Telegram and WhatsApp chat runs from the amoCRM interface. The manager does not jump between tabs; the lead sees all communication on the deal.

Pipeline automation

amoCRM robots take routine: auto-creating tasks on stage change, template emails and messages, stalled-deal reminders, automatic owner changes. A digital pipeline warms leads by launching ad shows and emails based on deal behavior.

Bots in amoCRM chats

amoCRM’s messenger-centric design is a native home for our AI bots. The bot answers the client in Telegram or WhatsApp, qualifies, and creates a deal; the manager picks up the dialogue from the card. Night requests are already in the pipeline by morning with answers to the first questions.

AI lead scoring

On top of amoCRM runs an AI sales agent: lead scoring from your deal history, call summaries into cards, stalled-deal control. Per an industry report, scoring lifts win rate by up to +76%.

How much it costs

The market range for basic small-business implementation is roughly 30–80k RUB. We price individually for the sales team. amoCRM plans are paid to the vendor and issued to you. The estimate locks after the sales team audit.

Why us

We are an independent integrator and implement both amoCRM and Bitrix24, so the recommendation depends on your tasks, not our margin. The adoption method includes training on live deals and first-weeks control, because statistics show 30–50% of CRM implementations fail exactly there.
