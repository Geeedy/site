AI will make a website for free: why pay a studio

Honest answer: for a draft and hypothesis check, an AI generator really works, and we use AI in development ourselves, like most engineering teams. But a generator will not choose structure for demand, will not connect CRM and 1C, does not own the outcome, and does not know your economics.

The boundaries are simple. Test the hypothesis with a generator or builder; when paid traffic starts and requests become money, you need architecture: integrations, speed, analytics. A detailed breakdown of paths is on the website with AI page.

How a website with AI differs from a regular one

A regular site shows information. A site with AI works with the visitor: a bot answers in seconds, smart search understands free-text queries, personalization shows what is relevant. Demand for “website with AI” reached 12.7k searches per month in our Wordstat measurements and keeps growing. More on the website with AI page.

How we work

  5. Support and growth. The site is improved from data.
