Turnkey Landing Page: a Page That Pays Back Clicks

An ad click costs money, and every visitor who leaves without a request takes that money with them. We build landings as part of a chain: an offer for a specific segment, a form that sends the request into CRM with a channel tag, analytics through to the lead. If a builder can solve your task in an evening, we will say so honestly.

Estimate a landing page Ask a question

When a landing, when a full site

The criterion is simple. One offer for one segment means a landing; several directions and services mean a corporate website. A landing concentrates the visitor on one action: the request. When offers compete for attention, conversion drops for all of them at once.

Choice logic is honest. Test a hypothesis with a builder or generator. When traffic is flowing and every lead costs money, custom pays back: higher conversion, full analytics, no platform limits.

What a custom landing includes

  • Offer and structure. Built for the queries and objections of a specific segment: result in the headline, proof in the blocks.
  • Copy. We write it, from your brief and product facts.
  • Form → CRM. The request lands in the funnel with a UTM tag so you see which channel pays back.
  • Analytics. Goals, events, call tracking when needed.
  • Speed and responsive. Fast load on mobile, where ad traffic lives.

Launch in 4 steps

  1. Offer and structure for the segment and its pains.
  2. Copy and design with proof instead of decoration.
  3. Form → CRM, requests arrive with channel tags.
  4. Analytics with goals and events through to the lead.

Cost and timelines

Market guide for a custom landing starts from roughly 120k RUB; builders cost roughly 0–50k RUB DIY. We price individually for offer and integrations. Typical timeline is 3–6 weeks from brief to launch. The estimate locks before kickoff.

Landing and SEO

Honestly: a landing is a tool for ad traffic, not for SEO. One page will not cover niche demand. If you need search traffic, look at a corporate website with structure for demand and SEO promotion. Landing and SEO site often work as a pair: ads go to the landing, organic to the site.

Why us

A landing is a tier on the development ladder: the same engineering culture, honest path choice, requests into CRM from day one. When you grow, the next tier is already designed.
