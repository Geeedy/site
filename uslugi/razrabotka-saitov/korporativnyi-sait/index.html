Corporate Website: A Working Sales Tool, Not a Bigger Brochure Site

A corporate website is needed when a company has several lines, and each must be found in search, explain value, and bring requests. We design structure for real demand, connect CRM and 1C, build in SEO from day one, and the site starts working for sales from launch.

What a corporate website includes

  • Structure for demand. Line pages are designed from real semantics, and each service catches its search demand.
  • Service catalog. With filters, attributes, and clear navigation.
  • Integrations. Requests to CRM with UTM tags, statuses from 1C, calls into the client card.
  • SEO readiness. Meta, markup, and speed so promotion can start on launch day.
  • AI modules by task. Consultant bot and smart search, as on the website with AI page.

Integrations: CRM, 1C, telephony

A mid-segment corporate site without integrations stays unfinished. Forms send requests to CRM with source and campaign tag, 1C provides current prices and availability, telephony writes calls into the card. The manager sees the client path end to end; the lead sees channel payback.

How much a corporate website costs

The market range for custom development with integrations is roughly 300–900k RUB; template solutions start from roughly 120–300k RUB; portals reach 1.6M+ RUB. We calculate our estimate individually by structure and integrations. Typical timeline is 2–3 months from brief to launch. The estimate locks after the prototype.

Process: 5 steps

  1. Brief and semantics. We match company lines with real demand and get site structure.
  2. Prototype. Pages and logic before design.
  3. Design and development. Iterations with demos.
  4. Integrations and content. CRM, 1C, texts by line.
  5. Launch and handoff. Domain, analytics, access yours.

Redesign of an old corporate site

If the site is technically healthy but visually outdated, we update design and structure without losing search rankings, with all redirects and preserved SEO weight. If the foundation is rotten, we will honestly show the calculation: sometimes a new site is cheaper than revival. The decision is yours, on audit numbers.

AI modules

A corporate site with AI works with the visitor itself: a bot answers service questions in seconds, smart search understands free-text queries, requests are qualified before the manager. More on the website with AI page.
