SEO Promotion: Leads That Get Cheaper Over Time

Ads work while you pay. Stop the budget, and traffic disappears. SEO works differently: the first months’ investment turns into a lead stream whose cost falls over time. We promote sites in Yandex and Google with honest timelines, analytics to the lead, and readiness for search with AI answers.

Free audit Estimate promotion

What SEO is and how it pays back

SEO promotion is work on the site and content so pages rank high for client queries and bring requests without pay-per-click. The main argument is economics: after reaching the top, an SEO lead costs roughly 300–700 RUB versus 800–7,000 RUB from contextual ads.

In competitive niches an ad click costs roughly 300–1,500 RUB in services and 500–2,500 RUB in B2B. SEO will not replace ads overnight, but over a year channel pairing cuts cost per lead by 40–60%, and organic drives the main savings.

Honest timelines: when to expect results

Read a “top in a month” promise as a red flag. Real corridors from analysis of Russian projects:

  • first rankings on long-tail queries come in 3–6 months;
  • a stable lead stream appears in 4–8 months;
  • top-10 on high-frequency queries for a new domain takes 6–12 months;
  • investment pays back in 6–9 months.

How to get into Yandex AI answers

Search is ceasing to be a list of links: Yandex AI-answer audience exceeded 13 million people a day, while global referral traffic from search fell a quarter year over year. Meanwhile query volume is not falling; classic snippet CTR is.

Practices for getting cited are known: page in top-20/30 for the query, a direct 40–60 word answer under the heading, question-style subheads, FAQPage and Service markup, content freshness, open AI crawlers. We apply this on every client page, including this one.

From practice. We measure SERP commercialization instrumentally, and in AI topics it is only 1–5%. That means expert content can take both classic top and AI-answer citations before competitors with budgets arrive.

Work plan by month

  1. Months 1–3: foundation. Audit, semantics, technical cleanup, structure, first texts.
  2. Months 4–6: growth. Content for clusters, behavioral factors, first long-tail rankings.
  3. Months 7–12: scale. High-frequency queries, expanded semantics, converting traffic to leads.

Why we do not promise the top

No honest contractor can guarantee a place in the SERP, because algorithms change weekly. A “pay for top” model pushes vendors toward behavioral-factor manipulation, and PF manipulation remains the main Yandex filter risk: a brief rise, then a filter and months of domain recovery.

We guarantee work volume and quality, transparent reporting, and an honest forecast, because that is controllable and verifiable. Rankings come as a consequence.

Analytics: rankings → traffic → leads

Rankings remain an intermediate metric. We report the chain “rankings → traffic → requests” on Yandex Metrica and Webmaster data. With end-to-end analytics you see the main thing: cost per SEO lead and its month-to-month dynamics.

How much SEO costs

A typical promotion budget on the Russian market is roughly 40–200k RUB per month depending on niche and competition.
