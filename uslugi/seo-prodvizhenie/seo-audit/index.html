SEO Audit: 10 Reasons Your Site Is Not Ranking

The site exists, but there are no search requests. The reason is always specific, and it can be found. We run the audit by hand, not with an automatic checker: we look at tech, content, demand, and readiness for the new SERP with AI answers. You leave with a report of priorities “what to fix first” and an honest forecast.

Send a request

What the audit checks

  1. Tech. Speed and Core Web Vitals, indexing, mobile version, duplicates and redirects.
  2. Semantics and structure. Whether the site covers real niche demand, whether pages compete with each other.
  3. Content. Quality, completeness, answers to user questions.
  4. Commercial factors. Prices, contacts, trust: everything Yandex evaluates on commercial sites.
  5. Link profile. Donor quality and toxicity.
  6. GEO readiness. Whether content will be cited in AI answers: markup, structure, llms.txt.

Checkers are useful for a quick self-check, but they will not say you are losing 70% of demand because pages for key clusters are missing, because they do not know your niche. An expert looks at the site through search-engine and business eyes at once.

GEO audit: will the site get into AI answers

Yandex AI answers are used by more than 13 million people a day, and classic SERP CTR is falling, because some clients get the answer without opening sites. We check whether your content is ready for citation: direct answers under headings, FAQPage and Service markup, freshness, AI crawler access. What GEO promotion is and how to get into neural answers has its own page. Most agencies’ audits lack this block, because the SERP changes faster than market checklists.

What you get

A report with findings laid out by priority. P1 shows what blocks growth right now, P2 what limits it, P3 what to improve as resources allow. Each finding comes with why it matters and what to do. Plus an honest forecast of realistic timelines and budget for your niche, with a link to SEO economics.

Common findings

From audit experience, “not ranking” causes repeat: no pages for key demand clusters (the site simply is not in the race), several pages competing for one query, slow mobile version, empty texts instead of answers to questions, traces of old manipulation pulling the site down. Your site will have its own list, specific and prioritized.

Free or full

The basic audit is free and includes main findings and priorities for your site. The full audit adds semantics, competitor review, and a work plan; we price it individually by site size. Start with free; it is enough to understand task scale and whether to work further.

What after the audit

Three paths, all honest. Fix yourselves from our report; it is specific enough for that. Order promotion with us, and the audit becomes the first month of work. Or it turns out the site is technically hopeless and a new one is cheaper; then we will show both calculations.

Why us

The audit is done by a team that builds sites and implements analytics itself, so we see not only SEO flags but the money behind them. The GEO block is built on our own method and data we measure instrumentally.
