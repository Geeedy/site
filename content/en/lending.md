# Turnkey Landing Page: a Page That Pays Back Clicks

An ad click costs money, and every visitor who leaves without a request takes that money with them. We build landings as part of a chain: an offer for a specific segment, a form that sends the request into CRM with a channel tag, analytics through to the lead. If a builder can solve your task in an evening, we will say so honestly.

## When a landing, when a full site

The criterion is simple. One offer for one segment means a landing; several directions and services mean a [corporate website](/uslugi/razrabotka-saitov/korporativnyi-sait/). A landing concentrates the visitor on one action: the request. When offers compete for attention, conversion drops for all of them at once.

## What a custom landing includes

- **Offer and structure.** Built for the queries and objections of a specific segment: result in the headline, proof in the blocks.

- **Analytics.** Goals, events, call tracking when needed.
- **Speed and responsive.** Fast load on mobile, where ad traffic lives.

## Cost and timelines

Market guide for a custom landing starts from roughly 120k RUB; builders cost roughly 0–50k RUB DIY. We price individually for offer and integrations. Typical timeline is 3–6 weeks from brief to launch. The estimate locks before kickoff.

## Landing and SEO

Honestly: a landing is a tool for ad traffic, not for SEO. One page will not cover niche demand. If you need search traffic, look at a [corporate website](/uslugi/razrabotka-saitov/korporativnyi-sait/) with structure for demand and [SEO promotion](/uslugi/seo-prodvizhenie/). Landing and SEO site often work as a pair: ads go to the landing, organic to the site.

## FAQ

**How much does a landing page cost?**
Market guide for custom from roughly 120k RUB. With a builder you can assemble for roughly 0–50k RUB DIY, and that is enough to test a hypothesis. We price individually. Calculation returns within 2 days after the brief.

**How long does it take?**
A custom landing takes 3–6 weeks: offer, copy, design, integrations, analytics. Faster on a builder or generator, with their limits. Timeline locks before kickoff.

**Landing or multi-page site?**
One offer for one segment means a landing. Several directions need a corporate website, otherwise offers compete for attention and conversion drops. Unsure? Describe the task; we will recommend honestly.

**Why is custom better than Tilda or an AI generator?**
Conversion and analytics: structure for your segment, copy for objections, full CRM and metrics integration, no platform limits. When every lead costs money, the difference pays back fast.

**Do you write the copy?**
Yes, from the brief and product facts, for the segment’s queries and objections. From you we need client knowledge and fact-checking. “I’ll place the texts myself later” does not work on a landing: the offer is the product.

**How do requests get into CRM?**
The form creates a deal with UTM source tags so you see which campaign brings leads and which burns budget. If there is no CRM, we can help with [implementation](/uslugi/vnedrenie-crm/), because without tracking leads get lost.

**Will the landing work for SEO?**
Limited: one page does not cover niche demand. A landing stays an ad-traffic tool. For organic you need a site with structure for demand, and we honestly advise not to expect SEO miracles from a landing.

**Do you run A/B tests?**
Yes, with enough traffic: headlines, offers, forms. On low traffic tests are not conclusive, so we first set up analytics and accumulate data, then test hypotheses.

**What do you need from us to start?**
Product, segment, prices, and client knowledge: 1–2 hours for the brief. The rest is our work. The more concrete you are about client pains, the stronger the offer.

**Can the landing grow into a full site later?**
Yes: the landing becomes the landing page for one direction, and structure builds around it. We design for growth: domain, analytics, and CRM integrations reuse without a rebuild.
