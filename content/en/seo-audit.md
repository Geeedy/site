## What the audit checks

[INFOGRAPHIC: ../assets/infographics/seo-audit-1.svg | What an SEO audit checks]

1. **Tech.** Speed and Core Web Vitals, indexing, mobile version, duplicates and redirects.
2. **Semantics and structure.** Whether the site covers real niche demand, whether pages compete with each other.
3. **Content.** Quality, completeness, answers to user questions.
4. **Commercial factors.** Prices, contacts, trust: everything Yandex evaluates on commercial sites.
5. **Link profile.** Donor quality and toxicity.
6. **GEO readiness.** Whether content will be cited in AI answers: markup, structure, llms.txt.

## FAQ

**What does an SEO audit include?**
Six blocks: tech (speed, indexing, mobile), semantics and structure, content, commercial factors, link profile, GEO readiness for AI answers. You leave with a report of P1/P2/P3 priorities and a forecast.

**How is an expert audit better than an online checker?**
A checker finds technical flags; an expert finds causes and money: demand gaps, cannibalization, weak content, priorities. A checker does not know your niche and will not say which pages are missing for growth.

**What is free and what is paid?**
The basic audit is free and includes main findings and priorities. Full adds semantics, competitors, and a work plan; we price individually by site size. Free is enough to understand task scale.

**How long does an audit take?**
Basic usually takes several business days; full with semantics and competitors up to two weeks. We name timelines at request; they depend on site size.

**What do I get in hand?**
A document with findings by priority, “why this matters” explanations, and concrete actions. Plus an honest timeline and budget forecast for your niche. The report is self-sufficient; you can fix without us.

**Do you need site access?**
For basic no; we work from open data. For full, Metrica and Webmaster access help; they show real traffic and sanctions. Admin access is not needed.

**Do you check readiness for AI answers?**
Yes, the GEO block is in every audit: answer structure, markup, freshness, AI crawler access. AI answers are used by 13+ million people a day; ignoring this channel is already expensive.

**How often should you audit?**
Full once a year or when strategy changes. Express checks quarterly, because algorithms and the SERP change. After major site updates, required, to check nothing broke.

**Does the audit oblige ordering promotion with you?**
No, the report is yours; fix yourselves or with any contractor. We earn from those who choose to work with us after the audit, and there are enough of them that we need not turn the audit into manipulation.

**Will you find the cause of a traffic drop?**
In most cases yes, because drops leave traces: dates in Metrica, site changes, algorithm updates, Webmaster sanctions. We will compare and name the most likely cause with a recovery plan.
