## AI will make a website for free: why pay a studio

Honest answer: for a draft and hypothesis check, an AI generator really works, and we use AI in development ourselves, like [most engineering teams](https://convergent.digital/blog/web-development-trends-2026). But a generator will not choose structure for demand, will not connect CRM and 1C, does not own the outcome, and does not know your economics.

The boundaries are simple. Test the hypothesis with a generator or builder; when paid traffic starts and requests become money, you need architecture: integrations, speed, analytics. A detailed breakdown of paths is on the [website with AI](/uslugi/razrabotka-saitov/sait-s-ii/) page.

## FAQ

**How much does a website cost: brochure, corporate, store?**
Market benchmarks: brochure and landing from roughly 120k RUB, corporate site roughly 300–900k RUB, store roughly 300–700k RUB and from 1.5M RUB for a systemic build. We price each task individually and return an exact estimate within 2 days after the brief.

**How long does development take?**
Brochure and landing take 3–6 weeks, corporate site and store 2–3 months, complex projects with integrations 4–6 months. Timelines lock in the contract after the prototype and do not drift.

**AI will make a website for free: why pay a studio?**
A generator is good for a draft and hypothesis check. It will not choose structure for demand, will not connect CRM and 1C, and does not own the outcome. When requests become money, you need architecture, and that is what you pay for.

**Tilda or custom development?**
Tilda is justified for a landing, MVP, and brochure site: fast and cheap. Limits hit on catalogs, 1C integrations, speed, and AI features. An honest breakdown with a table is on the [Tilda website](/uslugi/razrabotka-saitov/sait-na-tilde/) page.

**What technologies do you use?**
The stack is chosen for the task. The constants: speed, security, maintainability, and no lock-in to us. Code and access are yours; any contractor can continue the project.

**How does a “website with AI” differ from a regular one?**
A regular site shows; a site with AI answers and acts: a bot consults in seconds, smart search understands meaning, visitor data lands in CRM. That converts the traffic you already paid for with ads or SEO.

**What does support include?**
Monitoring, backups, updates, content edits, growth under SLA. Scope is by agreement, from a duty minimum to a dedicated team. Support is optional; the site is handed over fully.

**Will the site be ready for SEO promotion?**
Yes: structure for collected demand, meta tags, markup, speed, and correct indexing are standard. Promotion can start on launch day; more on the [SEO promotion](/uslugi/seo-prodvizhenie/) page.

**What is a microservices AI ecosystem and who needs it?**
A link of site, AI agents, CRM, and analytics as independent services. Needed when the site is embedded in processes: many requests, accounting integrations, processing automation. Built in stages, starting with site and bot.

**Do you improve existing sites?**
Yes: audit, improvements, integrations, AI modules. If the site is technically healthy, we grow it. Rebuild from scratch only when improvement costs more. The decision is yours, based on an audit with numbers.

**Who owns the code after the project?**
You do. Code, access, domain, analytics, and documentation are handed over fully. We earn on development and growth, not on holding you hostage.

**Do you integrate 1C and payments?**
Yes. Catalog and stock sync with 1C, payment systems, delivery, CRM, and telephony are standard for stores and corporate sites. Complex integrations are assessed on the prototype before the estimate locks.
