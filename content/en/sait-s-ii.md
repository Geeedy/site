The query “website with AI” gets 12.7 thousand impressions a month and keeps growing. By our Wordstat measurements it is the fastest-growing segment of web development. Behind the query are two different wishes: “let a neural network make me a website” and “I want a website that works with clients on its own.” We cover both paths honestly.

## What a website with AI is

A website with AI is a site where artificial intelligence works with the visitor: a bot answers questions in seconds, smart search understands free-word queries, content adapts to interests, and leads are qualified before they reach a manager. The site turns from a showcase into a process workplace. It does not just display; it serves and sells.

## Two paths: generate or order

First path: a neural network generates the site itself, that is structure, copy, and design. Fits a draft and hypothesis test. Second path: custom development of a site with AI features inside, such as a bot, search, personalization, and integrations. Fits when the site must bring leads and money. These are different tasks, and we will help you not mix them up.

## Path 1: a neural network makes the site

[IMG: ../assets/images/sait-s-ii-1.jpg | Neural network generating a site layout]

Generators assemble a landing from a text description: logo, structure, copy, images. For an MVP and niche test that is a working option, and we will not talk you out of it.

The limits of this path are known from market practice: a generator will not study demand and competitors, will not build structure for SEO, will not connect CRM and 1C, will not take responsibility for results. Engineering teams use AI differently: [about 90% of developers](https://convergent.digital/blog/web-development-trends-2026) generate a code skeleton that specialists then refine. AI speeds work; it does not replace design.

## FAQ

**What is a website with AI?**
A site where AI works with the visitor: a bot answers in seconds, search understands free wording, content personalizes, leads are qualified before the manager. The showcase becomes a sales workplace.

**A neural network will make a site for free: what’s the catch?**
No catch for a draft and hypothesis test; try it. The limits are elsewhere: a generator will not study demand, will not build SEO structure, will not connect CRM, and does not answer for results. When leads become money, you need engineering.

**Which AI features can be added to a website?**
A knowledge-base consultant bot, smart search, content personalization, dialogue lead qualification, auto-replies to typical emails. The set depends on the tasks. Usually start with a bot; it has the fastest effect.

**Can you add AI to an existing website?**
Yes, as modules without a rebuild: bot and search connect to a live site. That is a common scenario, because traffic is already there and the AI layer adds conversion. We check technical compatibility on a free audit.

**How much does a website with AI cost?**
Modules on an existing site we price individually by features. A new site is priced as the development step plus the AI layer. For comparison, a bot pays for itself in closed inquiries, and personalization lifts conversion 15–30% per industry data.

**What about user data (152-FZ)?**
Visitor dialogues and data are processed in your perimeter or on Russian platforms. Personal data does not leave for external services without your decision; architecture is built with 152-FZ in mind.

**Does AI content affect SEO?**
Yandex does not punish AI texts; quality decides. Meaningful content with facts and structure ranks regardless of how it was produced. A conveyor of meaningless pages is punished, and rightly so.

**How long does it take?**
A bot on an existing site usually takes from two weeks; most time goes to the knowledge base. A new site with an AI layer follows the development-step timeline; modules are built in parallel, not after.

**What does smart search give?**
The visitor finds what they need on the first query, because search understands meaning, typos, and synonyms. For catalogs and stores that is direct money: an unfound product means a purchase that did not happen.

**Where to start?**
With a free review. We look at the site and traffic and say which AI feature will pay off first. Usually that is a bot: fast launch, measurable effect, a base for other modules.
