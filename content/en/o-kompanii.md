# Engineers from Big Tech Who Count Your Money

We founded the company in 2023 with a simple idea: technology should cut the client’s costs, not inflate their budget. Three years later that is still the main filter for every decision we make: from choosing an AI model to website architecture.

[IMG: ../assets/images/o-kompanii-hero.jpg | Skill Dev team: engineers and ML specialists]

The team is distributed; we work with clients worldwide, and time zone or country do not matter.

## Our principles

Our principles are not page decoration; they are direct answers to three main client fears: “expensive and will not pay back,” “will not stick,” “they will lock us in.”

1. **The estimate is locked before kickoff** and does not grow retroactively; scope changes are discussed before, not after.
2. **Before-and-after metrics.** Every project starts by locking numbers and ends by comparing them; savings show in the report, not in a presentation.
3. **Code, access, and data stay yours.** We hand everything over: working with us further should be advantageous, not the only option.
4. **We do not take a project without payback.** If the audit shows AI will not pay off for you, we say so and propose what will.

## How we calculate savings

[IMG: ../assets/images/o-kompanii-1.jpg | Skill Dev engineers discussing project architecture]

The method is simple and checkable. We take a process and calculate its current cost: employee hours times rate, plus the cost of errors. We calculate automation cost: implementation plus support. The difference is your monthly savings, and their ratio gives payback time. The calculation is done before work starts on your numbers; after the pilot, plan is checked against fact, and the calculation becomes a report.

This method sits at the core of [AI implementation](/uslugi/vnedrenie-ii/), because we sell a calculated result, not technology.
