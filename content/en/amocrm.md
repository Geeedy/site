amoCRM is in the top three of the Russian CRM market and is strong in exactly one thing: sales team speed. Minimal extras, fast start, messengers right in the deal card. ## Who amoCRM fits

Sales teams of 2–20 people with short deals and active messenger chat: services, online schools, agencies, retail with requests. The platform is built for pipeline and speed, so it sticks more easily than heavy systems.

It does not fit companies that need tasks, documents, and a portal for all departments; there [Bitrix24](/uslugi/vnedrenie-crm/bitrix24/) is stronger. Vendor-neutral platform comparison is on the [CRM implementation page](/uslugi/vnedrenie-crm/).

## Channels into CRM

Requests from the [website](/uslugi/razrabotka-saitov/) land in the pipeline with source tags, calls are written into the card, Telegram and WhatsApp chat runs from the amoCRM interface. The manager does not jump between tabs; the lead sees all communication on the deal.

## Pipeline automation

amoCRM robots take routine: auto-creating tasks on stage change, template emails and messages, stalled-deal reminders, automatic owner changes. A digital pipeline warms leads by launching ad shows and emails based on deal behavior.

## Bots in amoCRM chats

amoCRM’s messenger-centric design is a native home for our [AI bots](/uslugi/chat-boty/). The bot answers the client in Telegram or WhatsApp, qualifies, and creates a deal; the manager picks up the dialogue from the card. Night requests are already in the pipeline by morning with answers to the first questions.

## FAQ

**How much does amoCRM implementation cost?**
Market range for basic implementation roughly 30–80k RUB for small business. We calculate implementation price individually. Platform plans are paid to the vendor separately. The estimate locks after the audit and does not grow during the work.

**How long does it take?**
Standard setup takes 1–2 weeks: pipeline, channels, migration, training. Complex 1C or telephony integrations add time. The sales team starts working in the system from week one.

**amoCRM or Bitrix24?**
amoCRM gives sales team speed, minimal extras, and messengers in the card. Bitrix24 covers whole-company processes: tasks, documents, portal. We implement both and recommend by tasks, in writing and with rationale.

**amoCRM plans: whose and how much?**
Plans are issued to you and paid to the vendor; price depends on user count and features. Current plans we show at audit. Our work (implementation and training) is paid separately.

**Which channels do you connect?**
Website (forms with UTM tags), telephony (calls into the card), Telegram, WhatsApp, email. All deal communication gathers in one card, and the manager works from one window.

**Migration from Excel or another CRM?**
Yes. Contacts, companies, and deal history transfer with relationships preserved and duplicates cleaned. The migration plan is agreed before kickoff. We clean a dirty base on intake instead of dragging chaos into the new system.

**How is the pipeline automated?**
With robots: auto-tasks, template messages, stage changes, stalled-deal reminders. A digital pipeline warms leads by behavior. We configure for your policies, not “like everyone else.”

**Do bots work in amoCRM?**
Yes, it is a strong pairing. An AI bot answers in Telegram and WhatsApp, qualifies, and creates a deal; the manager continues the dialogue from the card. Night requests are already in the pipeline by morning.

**What about AI scoring?**
An AI agent scores leads from your deal history and prioritizes the manager queue; hot ones go first. Per industry data, scoring lifts win rate by up to +76%. It connects after history accumulates in CRM.

**What if amoCRM becomes too small?**
That means the business grew, and migration to Bitrix24 or another platform is done with base and history preserved. We design implementation so data is portable and you are not locked into the platform.
