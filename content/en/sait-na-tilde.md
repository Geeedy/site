## 4 signals you have outgrown Tilda

[INFOGRAPHIC: ../assets/infographics/sait-na-tilde-2.svg | Four signals you have outgrown Tilda]

1. **The catalog does not fit.** Hundreds of products, filters lag, product pages are unmanageable.
2. **You need 1C integration.** Stock and prices can no longer be synced by hand.
3. **SEO hit the platform ceiling.** Speed and structure are not under your control.
4. **You need AI features.** A full [bot](/uslugi/chat-boty/), smart search, and personalization are not solved by widgets.

If two or more signals apply, it is time to estimate a migration. If only one, it may still be early, and we will say so.

## How to migrate off Tilda without losing rankings

[IMG: ../assets/images/sait-na-tilde-2.jpg | Migrating a site from Tilda to a custom platform]

Migration is not “build a new site and flip the domain.” We move structure and content, set redirects from old URLs to new ones, preserve accumulated search equity, and check indexing after the switch. A proper migration runs without a traffic crash, because old pages pass rankings to new ones.

## Is SEO on Tilda realistic

Partly. Basic optimization works: meta tags, structure, demand-focused copy, and for a brochure site or landing page that is enough. The ceiling is speed, structure flexibility, and technical control, because they belong to the platform, not you. Serious competition for contested rankings needs your own site; more in [SEO promotion](/uslugi/seo-prodvizhenie/). Unsure where your ceiling is? ## FAQ

**How much does a turnkey Tilda site cost?**
We quote our build individually for the job, plus the Tilda subscription under your account. For comparison, custom development starts from roughly 120k RUB on the market. An estimate for your job returns within 2 days.

**Tilda or custom development?**
Tilda fits a landing page, brochure site, and MVP: fast and cheap. Custom is needed when you need a catalog, 1C integrations, control of speed and SEO, and AI features. We do both; the recommendation is honest.

**What are Tilda's limits?**
A forever subscription, limited 1C and CRM integrations, a ceiling on catalog and load, speed and structure controlled by the platform, AI features only via widgets. For small jobs that does not matter; for growing ones it is critical.

**Is SEO on Tilda realistic?**
Basic yes: meta, structure, demand-focused copy. Enough for a brochure site and landing page. Contested rankings need control of speed and structure that a builder does not give; there you need your own site.

**Whose Tilda subscription, and what does it cost?**
The subscription is under your name, the site is yours, there is no lock-in to us. In the project estimate we show current platform plans and full cost of ownership with the subscription.

**Can you connect CRM to Tilda?**
Yes, basically: forms send requests into Bitrix24 or amoCRM with tags. Deep scenarios like catalog sync with 1C and complex funnels are limited on a builder, and that is one migration signal.

**How do you know it is time to migrate?**
Four signals: the catalog does not fit, you need 1C integration, SEO hit the platform ceiling, you need AI features. If two or more apply, estimate a migration. Send the site and we will say honestly whether it is time or too early.

**How do you migrate off Tilda without losing rankings?**
We move structure and content, set redirects from all old URLs, and control indexing after the switch. Rankings pass to new pages, and a proper migration runs without a traffic crash.

**How long does a Tilda site take?**
A landing page takes 1–2 weeks; a brochure or small site 2–3 weeks, with copy, analytics, and CRM connected. Nobody ships faster than a builder: that is the point.

**Do you build an AI bot for Tilda?**
Yes, as a widget: a knowledge-base bot connects to a Tilda site and passes leads into CRM. Full AI features like smart search and personalization need your own platform; more on a [website with AI](/uslugi/razrabotka-saitov/sait-s-ii/).
