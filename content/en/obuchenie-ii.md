# Corporate AI Training: the Team Learns on Your Processes, Not Someone Else’s Examples

An online course explains what neural networks are. Our workshop ends with a manager sending their first AI-assembled proposal, and an accountant closing inbound-mail triage in minutes. We teach employees to work with AI on your company’s policies and data, because training is part of [AI implementation](/uslugi/vnedrenie-ii/), not a lecture for a certificate.

## Why tools “do not take off” without training

Market figures describe the gap precisely. Per Sk Fintech Hub research in 2025, [54% of HR specialists are ready to delegate tasks to AI, but fewer than 30% actually know how to work with it](https://potok.io/blog/hr-research/vnedrenie-iskusstvennogo-intellekta-v-rabotu-nabiraet-temp/). An AI skills shortage holds back [49% of companies](https://ict.moscow/analytics/v-krupnykh-kompaniiakh-do-stadii-vnedreniia-doshli-do-10-pilotnykh-proektov-po-ii/). Companies buy subscriptions and roll out tools, while employees keep working the old way, and savings stay on paper. Training closes exactly that gap.

## How training runs

[INFOGRAPHIC: ../assets/infographics/obuchenie-ii-2.svg | Four steps of the training program]

1. **Role-task audit.** We find what an employee’s week consists of and what of that AI can handle.
2. **Tool setup.** We prepare prompts, templates, and access for department tasks before the session.
3. **Practice on real tasks.** Employees solve yesterday’s tasks with AI, not abstract examples.
4. **Reinforcement and measurement.** Homework on the live flow and comparison of time on task before and after.

## How we measure effect

[IMG: ../assets/images/obuchenie-ii-2.jpg | Reviewing training results at the board]

Before training we record time on 3–5 typical role tasks. Two weeks after training we measure the same tasks. The difference in hours, multiplied by the rate, is the training effect in money. Per hh.ru, learning and development with AI is already used by [17% of companies, and for 2026 +21% new rollouts are expected](https://www.novostiitkanala.ru/news/detail.php?ID=193494): the market is moving from talk to systematic skills work.

## What corporate training costs

Market ranges from provider offers in 2025–2026: a one-day workshop for a group of 15–25 people costs roughly 150,000–250,000 RUB; a modular 4–6 week program runs about 300,000–600,000 RUB per cohort. We assemble our price individually by number of roles and groups; after a short brief we prepare a personal offer. Inside implementation projects, training is included in the project estimate.

## Training inside implementation

The strongest format looks like this: a [process audit](/uslugi/vnedrenie-ii/audit-ii-strategiya/) finds where AI pays back, a pilot proves effect with metrics, and training hands tools to the team. Then skills land on a working solution, not in notes. For example, after launching a [chatbot](/uslugi/chat-boty/) we teach the team to expand the knowledge base, and bot accuracy grows without our involvement.

## FAQ

**How does corporate training differ from online courses?**
By material and result. A course teaches on generic examples and ends with a certificate; we teach on your company’s policies and data, and the employee leaves with tools tuned for their process. Effect is measured by time on task.

**Whom should we train first?**
The department with the most repeatable work on text and data. Usually sales, document workflow, or support. If an implementation is launching, the pilot-process team trains first so the solution sticks.

**What formats and duration?**
A 1-day workshop for a fast department start, a 2–4 week program with homework and reviews, or training inside an implementation project. Format is chosen for the task and team load.

**Are there role-based programs?**
Yes, that is the core of the approach: executives, sales, documents and accounting, HR, internal AI champions. Each program is built from tasks of a specific role in your company, not from a universal template.

**Do you teach on our data and processes?**
Yes; that is the main difference. Before the session we break down department tasks and prepare prompts and templates for them. In practice employees solve their real tasks, so tools keep working after the workshop.

**What does it cost?**
Market ranges: workshop 150,000–250,000 RUB per group, program 300,000–600,000 RUB per cohort. We assemble price individually for your team. In implementation projects training is in the estimate. Exact price depends on number of roles and groups.

**How is training effect measured?**
We record time on 3–5 typical role tasks before training and measure the same tasks two weeks after. The difference in hours, multiplied by the rate, shows effect in money. That is our “metrics before and after” standard.

**Can we order training separately from implementation?**
Yes, workshops and programs are sold separately. But the most durable effect comes from linking with implementation, when skills land on a working solution and are supported by it.

**What is a pilot group?**
The first group of 10–15 employees on which we calibrate the program for the company. Their results and feedback tune the material before scaling to other departments.

**What happens after training?**
Materials, prompts, and templates stay with the team. AI champions support use inside departments. By agreement we run monthly Q&A reviews until the skill becomes habit.

**Do employees need technical preparation?**
No; programs are designed for ordinary users. Confident browser and work-system use is enough. Technical roles get a separate track when needed.
