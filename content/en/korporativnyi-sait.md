## FAQ

**How does a corporate website differ from a brochure site?**
By task scale. A brochure presents the company; a corporate site sells lines. A corporate site has structure for each service’s search demand, a catalog, CRM and 1C integrations. It generates and processes requests, not just shows a phone number.

**How much does a corporate website cost?**
Market range for custom development with integrations roughly 300–900k RUB; template solutions from roughly 120k RUB; portals from roughly 1.6M RUB. We price individually. Exact estimate returns in 2 days after the brief; it locks after the prototype.

**How long does development take?**
Typically 2–3 months: semantics and prototype, design, development, integrations, content, launch. Timeline locks in the contract after the prototype. You can speed up with staged launch, starting with key lines.

**Which integrations are possible?**
CRM (Bitrix24, amoCRM), 1C (prices, availability, invoices), telephony, email services, maps, payment systems. Requests land in the pipeline with UTM tags, and channel payback is visible. Complex integrations are assessed on the prototype.

**Which CMS do you use?**
The platform is chosen for the task. Criteria are constant: speed, security, editing convenience, no lock-in to us. Code and access are handed over fully.

**Who fills the site with content?**
We do. Line texts are written for demand and go through SEO prep. Your role is limited to service facts and review. After launch your employee can edit content without a programmer.

**Is SEO included in development?**
SEO readiness is included: structure for demand, meta, markup, speed. Promotion as ongoing work is a separate [SEO](/uslugi/seo-prodvizhenie/) service that, on our sites, starts without a “fix the foundation” stage.

**Can you redesign an old corporate site?**
Yes. If the technical base is alive, we update design and structure while preserving rankings, including redirects and SEO-weight transfer. If the base is hopeless, we will show a new-site calculation, and you decide.

**Is multilingual needed?**
If you work with foreign clients, we build it in from the start, because proper multilingualism affects architecture. If there are no plans, do not overpay. Adding a language later is possible; we design with that in mind.

**What about AI modules?**
A service consultant bot, smart search, and request qualification connect as modules at development or later. On a corporate site a bot is especially useful: it answers questions by line while managers are busy.
