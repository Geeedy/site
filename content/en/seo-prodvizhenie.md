# SEO Promotion: Leads That Get Cheaper Over Time

Ads work while you pay. Stop the budget, and traffic disappears. SEO works differently: the first months’ investment turns into a lead stream whose cost falls over time. We promote sites in Yandex and Google with honest timelines, analytics to the lead, and readiness for search with AI answers.

## What SEO is and how it pays back

SEO promotion is work on the site and content so pages rank high for client queries and bring requests without pay-per-click. The main argument is economics: after reaching the top, [an SEO lead costs roughly 300–700 RUB versus 800–7,000 RUB from contextual ads](https://secrets.tbank.ru/blogi-kompanij/seo-vs-kontekstnaya-reklama/).

[INFOGRAPHIC: ../assets/infographics/seo-prodvizhenie-1.svg | Cost per lead: SEO vs contextual ads]

In competitive niches an ad click costs [roughly 300–1,500 RUB in services and 500–2,500 RUB in B2B](https://seobomba.ru/seo-vs-kontekstnaya-reklama-kuda-vlozhit-byudzhet-v-usloviyah-peregretogo-rynka/). SEO will not replace ads overnight, but over a year [channel pairing cuts cost per lead by 40–60%](https://webvozdux.ru/seo-vs-kontekstnaja-reklama-sravnenie-dlja-biznesa/), and organic drives the main savings.

## How to get into Yandex AI answers

Search is ceasing to be a list of links: Yandex AI-answer audience exceeded 13 million people a day, while global referral traffic from search fell a quarter year over year. Meanwhile [query volume is not falling; classic snippet CTR is](https://skillbox.ru/media/marketing/doli-yandeksa-i-google-v-rossii-chto-izmenilos-k-2025-godu/).

Practices for getting cited are known: page in top-20/30 for the query, a direct 40–60 word answer under the heading, question-style subheads, FAQPage and Service markup, content freshness, open AI crawlers. We apply this on every client page, including this one.

**From practice.** We measure SERP commercialization instrumentally, and in AI topics it is only 1–5%. That means expert content can take both classic top and AI-answer citations before competitors with budgets arrive.

## Why we do not promise the top

No honest contractor can guarantee a place in the SERP, because algorithms change weekly. A “pay for top” model pushes vendors toward behavioral-factor manipulation, and [PF manipulation remains the main Yandex filter risk](https://exiterra.ru/blog/prodvizhenie-sajta/obnovlenie-algoritmov-yandeksa-ii-teksty-mozhno-a-nakrutka-pf-rabotaet/): a brief rise, then a filter and months of domain recovery.

We guarantee work volume and quality, transparent reporting, and an honest forecast, because that is controllable and verifiable. Rankings come as a consequence.

## FAQ

**How soon will there be results?**
First rankings on long-tail queries come in 3–6 months, a stable lead stream in 4–8 months, top on high-frequency for a new domain in 6–12 months. We will be more precise after the audit, because starting conditions differ strongly.

**How much does promotion cost?**
Market range roughly 40–200k RUB per month by niche. We assemble the plan individually. Work scope locks monthly; the report shows what was done and dynamics: rankings, traffic, requests.

**How is SEO better than contextual ads?**
Economics over distance: an organic lead costs roughly 300–700 RUB versus 800–7,000 RUB from ads, and traffic does not vanish when the budget stops. The downside: results are not instant. We do not set up ads, but channels do not conflict, and pairing cuts cost per lead by 40–60% in 6–8 months.

**Yandex or Google: where to focus?**
On Yandex, which has about 74% of Russia’s search traffic versus 25% for Google. We optimize for both, but work priorities are Yandex factors: behavioral, commercial, regionality.

**What are AI answers and how do you get into them?**
They are neural answers right in the Yandex SERP; 13+ million people use them daily. To get cited you need top-20/30 for the query, direct 40–60 word answers, question headings, markup, and freshness. This optimization (GEO) is part of our work.

**Why do you not guarantee rankings?**
No contractor controls rankings; algorithms change weekly. “Top guarantees” lead to behavioral-factor manipulation, the main Yandex filter risk. We guarantee work, timelines, and reporting to leads, because that is verifiable.

**Can you use AI-written texts?**
Yes. Yandex does not punish AI content per se; quality and usefulness decide. We use AI in content production with editing and facts from primary sources: machine speed, human responsibility.

**What work happens by month?**
Months 1–3 cover audit, semantics, tech, and structure. Months 4–6 deliver content, behavioral factors, and first rankings. Months 7–12 bring high-frequency queries and conversion. Monthly scope locks in the contract.

**Will you promote a site you did not build?**
Yes. We start with an audit, fix the technical base, then work as usual. If the site is technically hopeless, we will show the calculation honestly: sometimes a new SEO-ready site is cheaper than endless patches.

**Can you speed up SEO?**
Partly. Long-tail queries, technical cleanup, and re-optimizing existing pages give the fastest effect. Forcing high-frequency top without grey methods is not possible, and grey methods lead to filters whose recovery costs more than the honest path.

**What happens if we stop promotion?**
Rankings will not zero overnight; content and authority keep working. But competitors do not stop, and over time rankings slip. Restarting is cheaper than starting from scratch, because the foundation remains.

**How do you know SEO is working?**
By the metric chain in the monthly report: ranking growth by clusters → traffic growth in Metrica → request growth. With end-to-end analytics you see cost per SEO lead, and it should fall quarter to quarter.
