Privacy and Cookie Policy

This policy explains what data the Skill Dev website collects, why we process it, on what basis, and how you can control it. Data controller: [legal entity name, registration number, registered address]. Effective date: [add date].

What data we process

We process two types of data. The first you provide yourself when you submit a request or contact us: name, phone, email, and the text of your message. The second the site collects automatically through cookies and analytics: anonymized information about the visit, device and browser type, referral source, and pages viewed.

Why we process data

Data from the form lets us reply to your request, prepare an estimate, and contact you as you asked. Anonymized analytics data helps us understand how the site works and make it more convenient. We do not use your data for purposes not described in this policy.

Legal basis

We process personal data in line with applicable data protection law. The basis for processing form data is your consent, which you give by submitting the request. The basis for analytics cookies is your consent, which you confirm by continuing to use the site.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files the site stores in your browser. We use technical cookies, without which the site does not work correctly, and analytics cookies that help us measure traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings or decline them in the consent banner. Some site features may then work worse.

Analytics

To analyze traffic, the site uses Yandex Metrica. It collects anonymized data about visitor behavior and does not allow identifying a specific person. Analytics data is processed on the service side under its own terms.

Sharing data with third parties

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for their own purposes. Data may be processed by our contractors who help deliver services, only to the extent needed for the work and under confidentiality terms.

How long we keep data

We keep request data for as long as needed to handle your inquiry and meet our obligations to you, then delete or anonymize it. Analytics data is stored in anonymized form in Yandex Metrica.

Your rights

You may request access to your data, its correction or deletion, and withdraw your consent to processing. To do so, write to us at hello@skill-dev.ai. We will respond within the period set by law.

How we protect data

We apply organizational and technical measures to protect data from unauthorized access, alteration, and loss. Only employees who need it for their work have access to request data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The current version is always published on this page, and the effective date is shown at the top of the document.

Contact

For questions about data processing, write to hello@skill-dev.ai.
